# ChatGPT

## Overview
ChatGPT is OpenAI's conversational AI assistant, built on GPT-3.5 (initial release) and later GPT-4. It was released in November 2022 and reached 100M users within two months.

## Key Capabilities
- Natural conversational interaction
- Instruction following
- Code generation and explanation
- General knowledge问答

## Performance on Logical Reasoning
In the GPT-4 logical reasoning evaluation:
- Outperforms fine-tuned RoBERTa on most benchmarks
- GPT-4 > ChatGPT on most tasks
- Both struggle significantly on out-of-distribution and NLI tasks
